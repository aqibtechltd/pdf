How to Merge PDF Files:

  1. Drop your PDF files in the upload area or click "Choose Files"
  2. Select multiple PDF files you want to merge
  3. Drag and drop files to arrange them in the desired order
  4. Click "Merge PDFs" to combine the files
  5. Download your merged PDF file
Note: All processing is done in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
