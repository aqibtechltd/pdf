How to Rotate PDF Pages:

  1. Upload your PDF file by dropping it or clicking "Choose File"
  2. Use the rotation buttons on each page to:
    • Rotate left (90° counterclockwise)
    • Rotate right (90° clockwise)
  3. Use bulk actions to rotate all pages at once
  4. Click "Save PDF" to download your rotated document
Note: All processing is done in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
