How to Split PDF Files:

  1. Upload your PDF file by dropping it or clicking "Choose File"
  2. Select your preferred split method:
    • Extract Pages: Select specific pages to extract
    • Split by Range: Split into custom page ranges
    • Split Every N Pages: Split into equal parts
  3. Configure your split options
  4. Click "Split PDF" to process your file
  5. Download your split PDF files
Note: All processing is done in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
